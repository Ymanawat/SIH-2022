Frequenty Asked Questions

How to upload your project?

Firstly, You login to the website with your credentials, then you can upload your project using publish button and then a request is sent to your college, if approved, then it is automatically published.

Can i publish without approval?

No, your college approval is required.

How to connect with the student who created project?

you can connect with the student with your email-id. You can ask your any query regarding that project.

How can you access learning resources of the project?

Just find the right project you want to learn from and then go to the project description and get the learning resources there.

Who is eligible to upload project?

Students from any stream can upload their projects.
